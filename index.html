How to Use Manglish Online?

It’s New.It’s Fast.It’s Easy.

English to Malayalam typing

Type using English letters in the box. Choose one of the Malayalam words using your mouse or with keyboard arrow keys.

Voice typing in Malayalam

Click on the ‘Voice Typing’ button and speak the Malayalam words to your microphone. The spoken words will appear on the screen automatically. It's 2x faster than typing!

Copy & Paste

Click on the ‘Copy Text’ button and paste the Malayalam text on WhatsApp Web, Facebook, Instagram, Microsoft Word or anywhere else.
